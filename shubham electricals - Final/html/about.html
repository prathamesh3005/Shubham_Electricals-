About Us >>
SEECO: Pioneers in Sustainable Energy Solutions
SEECO was established by a team of visionary engineers who recognized India's pivotal role in global energy conservation and environmental protection. Drawing on our extensive experience and unwavering dedication, SEECO has emerged as a leader in the provision of comprehensive solar services, encompassing solar EPC, solar O&M, and the construction of transformer substations. Our expertise extends to LT and HT work, encompassing cable laying and various maintenance tasks, particularly in the context of M.S.E.D.C.L. Sanction processes and liaison work, as well as net metering services. In addition to our electrical prowess, SEECO takes pride in being a foremost manufacturer of RCC Half-round pipes. Our dedicated team of experts, many of whom have conducted groundbreaking research in solar technology during their academic careers, forms the backbone of our operations. We have established a specialized training unit committed to upholding the highest standards across all facets of our work. SEECO is your trusted partner in sustainable energy solutions, driven by a passion for excellence and a commitment to a cleaner, more energy-efficient future.

MISSION

At SEE, our mission is to facilitate the shift towards clean and sustainable energy systems. We achieve this by delivering exceptional solar services, transformer substation work, and innovative electrical solutions. With a focus on quality and environmental consciousness, we aim to be a trusted partner in creating a greener future. Through our expertise and commitment to excellence, we strive to meet the unique needs of our clients while contributing to a more sustainable world.

VISION

Our vision at SEE is to lead the way in transforming the energy sector. We strive to be the preferred choice for solar services, transformer substation work, and advanced electrical solutions. By embracing innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction, we aim to drive the widespread adoption of renewable energy technologies. Through our efforts, we envision making a significant positive impact on the industry, communities, and the environment, paving the way for a cleaner and more sustainable future.

ACHIEVEMENTS

SEE has achieved remarkable milestones in the industry, including the successful completion of diverse projects such as HT and LT substations, transformer installations, solar EPC, and O&M services. With a strong focus on customer satisfaction, we have built long-term relationships with our clients. As a Licensed Electrical Contractor, we ensure compliance with regulations. Our commitment to innovation and technological expertise sets us apart, leading to industry recognition. We continue to strive for excellence and push boundaries in the renewable energy sector.
