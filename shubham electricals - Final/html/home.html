SEE (Shubham Electricals and Engineering Co.) is a government-authorized company that specializes in providing top-notch electrical services and solar panel solutions. With prestigious vendorship from Government organizations like MSEB (MSEDCL), the Indian Navy base, and GeM, SEE has a proven track record of successfully executing tendering projects. With a focus on quality, reliability, and sustainability, SEE has expanded its business into the private sector as well by offering comprehensive electrical services and innovative solar panel implementation and maintenance. With a team of skilled professionals and a commitment to excellence, SEE is a trusted partner for clients seeking efficient and eco-friendly electrical solutions.



Featured Work >>



01. RCC Half-Round Pipe >>

SEE is a trusted manufacturer of affordable RCC half-round pipes, specifically designed for the protection of underground cables. We ensure the highest quality by conducting thorough tests such as pressure, break, and vibration testing. With our reliable and reasonably priced pipes, clients can have peace of mind knowing their underground cables are well-protected.

02. Solar Work >>

SEE is a top-tier O&M solution provider with expertise in solar project design and development. We offer comprehensive services under one roof, allowing IPPs and EPCs to focus on their core areas. Our specialized approach caters to rooftop solar projects, providing cost-effective, customized maintenance solutions. With preventive maintenance as a priority, we ensure optimal plant performance and minimize downtime for PV systems. Trust SEE for reliable and efficient O&M services.

03. Transformer Substation LV & HV >>

SEE excels in supplying, erecting, testing, and commissioning HT and LT substations. Our skilled workers handle all types of transformer work, including pole-mounted or plinth-mounted installations, along with the necessary protective equipment. With extensive experience managing switchyards from 230V and beyond, we prioritize safety and minimize downtime by adhering to best practices, equipment manuals, grid codes, and our own Standard Operating Procedures. As a licensed electrical contractor in multiple states, SEE ensures compliance with statutory requirements. Count on us for superior services and industry-leading standards.

04. Net Metering & Liasoning Work, MSEB Related Work >>

SEE boasts exceptional skills in handling various tasks related to Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL). We excel in tasks such as obtaining sanctions, liaising with authorities, executing new and existing meter work, and carrying out load extension projects. As an authorized vendor of MSEDCL, we have the privilege of participating in tenders and providing our expertise in these areas. Count on SEE for seamless execution and outstanding service as we navigate the intricacies of MSEDCL projects.
